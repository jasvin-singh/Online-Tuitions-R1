Fun Ways to Learn French (Grades 4 to 9)

Activity Fun Learning Tips
1. Listen to Songs
  • Play simple French songs or rhymes for kids
  • Try to sing along and learn new words with rhythm
2. Watch Cartoons in French
  • Watch your favorite cartoons with French audio
  • Use English subtitles at first, then try without them!
3. Flashcards & Colors
  • Make colorful flashcards for French words (e.g., animals, food)
  • Play memory games with them to remember faster
4. Practice Speaking
  • Learn simple sentences to introduce yourself and talk about family or hobbies
  • Practice with friends or in front of a mirror
5. Play Vocabulary Games
  • Play games like bingo or word match with French words
  • Use fun apps like Duolingo for kids
6. Read Picture Books
  • Read easy French storybooks with pictures
  • Point at pictures and say the words aloud in French
7. Draw and Label
  • Draw things like fruits, animals, or a house
  • Label them with French names and hang on your wall!
8. Learn a Word a Day
  • Write down one new French word each day
  • Try to use it in a sentence or in a drawing
